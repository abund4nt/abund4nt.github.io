About

My interests span across web and mobile security, reverse engineering, and low-level exploitation. I’m constantly learning, exploring new techniques, and pushing my skills further in this ever-evolving field.

I’m highly driven by innovative solutions and enjoy tackling challenges with a creative mindset. I work fast and efficiently, often finding practical paths where others see obstacles. My ability to quickly analyze and adapt allows me to deliver high-impact results in short timeframes, making me a productive and resourceful member of any team.
